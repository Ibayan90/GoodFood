Kitchens of the World

French cuisine :
is the style of cooking derived from the French people,
and it is the result of centuries of social and political
changes, French cuisine in the Middle Ages was famous
for its luxurious ornate tables used by the upper classes,
most of the seasonal French foods were prepared
by chefs like Gillam Terel, who lived in the era
of the French Revolution.

Greek cuisine:
It is considered one of the most delicious
cuisines of the European continent for the taste
of its delicious foods, as it shares some cooking
features with the kitchens of the Middle East countries.
Most of Greek cuisine is added to olives and vegetables.

Turkish cuisine :
Turkish cuisine has a long history,
as it has been influenced over time
by Kurdish, Persian, Indian, Arab and Armenian
cuisine. This influence was a natural result of
the emergence of the Ottoman Empress and her control
over many countries in the eastern Mediterranean
region over about 500 years.

Saudi cuisine :
Saudi cuisine varies according to the
geographical area or region, so Saudi
cuisine is wide and varied, and it is divided
into several kitchens according to the
geographical area, there is the Najdi kitchen in
the Najd region, the Hijaz kitchen in the Hijaz region,
the Ahsai kitchen in Al-Hasa,
and the southern kitchen in the southern region

Cooking Methods

Grilling:

Through this method, food is cooked at high temperatures on charcoal or ceramic stone, which leads to the browning of different foods and giving them a special flavor and taste, and the degree of maturity can be controlled by bringing the food closer, or removing it from the source of heat..

Deep frying:

Food is cooked in this way by placing it inside hot oil, where different foods are cooked due to the high temperature of the oil, and this method gives a special shape and flavor to the foods, in addition to the crunch that you add to the foods.

Roasting:

so that different foods are cooked inside the oven by frying the foods by hot air that circulates around the food, and this method is suitable for different foods, especially foods that need a long time to cook in addition to large pieces of meat
